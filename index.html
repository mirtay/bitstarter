Worrying is time consuming and rarely productive. When your to-do list grows long and overwhelming, it's difficult to prioritize and easy to become unnecessarily stressed. Everyone needs a voice of reason from time to time.

Leave your stress with me and go get stuff done! Leave it with me provides an easy to use organizational framework for generating an action plan. The system takes into consideration scope, timing, and mind space to produce an plan that is optimized to reduce your overall anxiety level. Simply glance at the heatmap to see how anxious you should actually feel at any given time. If the heat is on, go to your action plan, get to work and watch as your anxiety level falls.
